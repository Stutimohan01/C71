Ruby Jubliee

Best Auditorium

The staff conference scheduled to be held in Ruby Jubilee Auditorium was organized in different classrooms following the social distancing norms due to the outbreak of Covid-19. Sister congratulated the teachers for the successful completion of the session 2019-2020. The results for all the classes had been declared in the previous week and the teachers were given a timetable for the classes for the session 2020-21. Since Novel Corona Virus was on the rise, it was decided to reschedule the opening of the school for rest of the classes from April 1, instead of 19th March. Teachers were instructed to make online assignments for the students.
